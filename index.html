ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Paulina, a cross-disciplinary designer with love for technology.

I create projects that balance usability, practicality, and memorable design. Over five years at Ocean Technologies, as well as through freelance collaborations, I led and completed numerous projects, working closely with Design and IT teams.

I supplemented my Digital Design major with Front-End postgraduate studies and am currently pursuing a Python/Django/AI bootcamp to deepen my understanding of the applications I design and enhance my cross-field collaborations.

I also enjoy mentoring junior designers, experimenting with 3D/2D motion graphics, and exploring interactive web experiences.

My tools of choice are; Adobe Suite, Blender, Cinema4D and Figma.
